## Name

Doom Stalling

## Summary (short, plain text)

Stop doomscrolling. A gentle countdown gives you time to reconsider before entering time-wasting sites.

## Description

You know the feeling. You open Reddit "just for a second" and suddenly it's been two hours. Doom Stalling is a simple intervention: when you navigate to a site you've flagged as unproductive, it pauses you with a countdown and a pointed question.

It doesn't block anything. It just gives you a moment to ask yourself: "Do I actually want to do this?"

Features:
• Configurable list of sites to intercept
• Adjustable countdown timer (1–600 seconds)
• Random motivational questions that change each time
• Global on/off toggle when you need a break from the breaks
• Clean, warm design — not another dark-mode neon blocker
• All settings auto-save
• Zero data collection — everything stays in your browser

Doom Stalling is open source. Report issues, suggest features, or contribute at:
https://github.com/lovromazgon/doom-stalling

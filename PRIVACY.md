# Privacy Policy — Doom Stalling

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

## Summary

Doom Stalling does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. Period.

## Data storage

Doom Stalling stores the following data locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage Sync API:

- Your list of blocked sites
- Your countdown delay setting
- Whether the extension is enabled or disabled

This data is synced across your Chrome browsers if you are signed into Chrome with sync enabled. It is never sent to any external server.

## Data collection

Doom Stalling does **not** collect:

- Browsing history
- Personal information
- Analytics or usage data
- Cookies or tracking identifiers

## Third-party services

Doom Stalling uses Google's public favicon service (`google.com/s2/favicons`) to display site icons in the settings page. This request includes the domain name of sites you have added to your block list. No other data is sent to any third party.

## Permissions

- **storage**: Used to save your settings (site list, delay, enabled state)
- **webNavigation**: Used to detect navigation to blocked sites and show the intervention page

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here with a new "last updated" date.
